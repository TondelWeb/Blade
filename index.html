Our Story

More than a barbershop.
A sanctuary.

Blade & Co. was born from one belief: that every man deserves a place where craft meets calm. We opened our doors in 2018 in the heart of Manhattan with a simple promise — no rushed cuts, no corners cut, no compromises.

Our team of master barbers brings decades of combined experience, a passion for precision, and a genuine interest in making every client feel like the only person in the room.

Walk-ins welcome, appointments preferred
